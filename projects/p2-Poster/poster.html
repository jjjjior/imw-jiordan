Since 1985, Indianapolis will host NBA All-Star Weekend from February 16-18, 2024. To honor basketball’s deep roots in the state of Indiana, The 73rd All-Star game will return to the classic “East vs. West” matchup as well as the traditional game of four 12-minute quarters with no target score or untimed fourth quarters.
